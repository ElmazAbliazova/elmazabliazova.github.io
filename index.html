About Me

I'm a second-year Biomedical Engineering student at Case Western Reserve University on the Biomechanics track, with a particular interest in prosthetics and orthotics.

I currently work at the Sears think[box] Makerspace, where I gain hands-on experience in design and fabrication while also training and teaching others to use the equipment safely and effectively.

I'm choosing a path at the intersection of engineering and medicine, but my curiosity constantly pushes me to explore far beyond it.
